Kuiper Assult

A modern asteroids style game, but on steroids. Designed in beautiful full HD.

Over 46 Levels

Packed with twelve stages and 46 plus levels that obviously get progressively harder with each one. Upgradable ship with up to five weapon systems.

Are you up for the task?

Only a top notch pilot will do. We cannot and must not fail - the fate of the entire race is at stake.

Everything on your ship uses energy. That being said, it is a Star Fighter and has has the same Hybrid system the Prius uses. It will recharge the batteries, just lay off the guns a bit and stop panicking!

You can also pickup the power ups you see spawn around you and they will randomly give you energy or armour. You ship has shields and can take a beating.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Medium Powered Desktop PC
  • Fullscreen Mode
  • Click Play Now Button
