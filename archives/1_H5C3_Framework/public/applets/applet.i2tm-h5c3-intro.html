H5C3 Framework is arriving...

After many months of development we have a HTML5 & CSS3 compliant Framework that provides a way of creating CloudApps that work on Mobile devices, Desktops and Even Game consoles.

We have designed the H5C3 Framework around the Google Chromium Framework. No other browser is currently supported at this time. The reason for this is quite simple...Google Chrome just works, flawlessy with all W3C standards. Microsoft Internet Explorer, Apple Safari and the others all issues and or bugs which even though a work around could be done...it would diminish your overall experience with our services and applications.

What is a CloudApp?

CloudApps are are born, live thier lives and die on the server. There is no need for the developers to maintain computer systems, backups, write installers, updaters, etc. The DevPortal provides for Project management, version tracking, support desk, Online IDE, Bulding, Sandbox, everything a developer needs.

Platforms & Devices Supported

  • iOS 4.3 & Later
  • Android 4.0 & Latar
  • Windows (All)
  • Mac OS X 10.6 or later
  • Ubuntu 10.04+
  • Debian 6+
  • OpenSuSE 11.3+
  • Fedora Linux 14


Advanced Users Can also Play use our services on these platforms

  • Xbox 360
  • Playstation 3
  • Wii U
  • Wii (Possible...Some signs of issues on the forums)

FOR THE DEV's!
The entire framework was designed to be developer friendly. We provide you with the resources and support you need, We certifiy the WebApp (including ESRB if a game), We publish and then we handle the financial side for you, just tell us where to deposit your $$$.
